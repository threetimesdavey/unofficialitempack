This document records the people whose work has shaped
**BG2:EE Unofficial Item Pack** and its community-maintained repository.

The original mod and the GitHub maintenance project are related, but they are
not the same act of authorship. The distinctions below are intentional.

## Original creator

### [Edvin](https://forums.beamdog.com/profile/Edvin)

- **Role:** Original author and creator of BG2:EE Unofficial Item Pack
- **Beamdog profile:** [Edvin](https://forums.beamdog.com/profile/Edvin)
- **Original discussion:** [BG2:EE — Unofficial Item Pack](https://forums.beamdog.com/discussion/56567/mod-bgii-ee-unofficial-item-pack/p1)

[Edvin](https://forums.beamdog.com/profile/Edvin) created the original project, including its concept, item designs,
component structure, WeiDU implementation, written material, original releases,
and English and Czech support.

The historical public release line through version `2.7b` is [Edvin](https://forums.beamdog.com/profile/Edvin)'s work
except where an individual historical contribution is explicitly documented.

[Edvin](https://forums.beamdog.com/profile/Edvin) must remain prominently credited in this repository, its releases, and
derived documentation as the original author.

Community maintenance does not transfer or diminish that authorship.
